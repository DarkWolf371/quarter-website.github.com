There's No Place Like Home

Quarters Real Estate Services is your complete resource for real estate and rental services in the Piedmont/Triad Area of North Carolina. We specialize in connecting buyers and sellers, tenants, and investment property owners in the Winston-Salem region. We also manage single-family and multi-family properties and HOAs in the area.

Our company has rapidly grown since 2009 based on our exceptional personal service and use of technology to streamline the process for all stakeholders. Our goal is to make your real estate experience a hassle-free, pleasant one.

For investment property owners, we protect your investment using our network of efficient vendors. We work diligently to minimize vacancies and keep you connected to your property through an online portal. Contact us to discuss your property.

For buyers and sellers, we work with urgency to help you achieve your real estate goals. It all starts by learning more in our Real Estate Services area and by contacting us for a free no-obligation initial consultation.

Discover why more tenants, investment property owners, buyers, sellers and HOAs love Quarters Real Estate Service. We look forward to assisting you.
